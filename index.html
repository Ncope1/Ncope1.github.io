The Misadventures of 808's & Dookie

This application was created as a group project, and geared toward music lovers who appreciate listening to an artist's or band's entire album to gain a full perspective of the audible impact the artist or band was aiming for. They can then post their own review of the album or create a review for a different album. The technologies used are: Express, Mongoose, MongoDB, JSON, Heroku, mLab, React, Axios, node-spotify-api npm package, and Bootstrap. Spotify's API was used as well as a Random Name Generator for JavaScript (c) by Thomas Konings.
