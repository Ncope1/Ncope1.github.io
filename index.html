Queen Bey Trivia

Once the user selects an answer based on the options provided, the game progresses to a new question along with a new set of answer options. For each correct answer that is selected a one point score is recorded.

Fab Nightly Skincare Routine

For this project I decided to make a skin care routine app (facial skincare) to provide users with information on how to properly care for their skin on a daily basis. The app was created using NodeJS, Handlebars, Materialize, Mongoose, Express, and Heroku. Users are able to create new skin care routines, review the routines that have already been added to the app, update current routines and also delete outdated routines.

The Misadventures of 808's & Dookie

This application is for music lovers that appreciate listening to an artist’s or band’s entire album and getting a full perspective for the audible impact the artist or band was going for. A music lover can then write a review of their thoughts on the album and post a picture of the album cover with their review. Other music lovers can read their review, decide whether or not to listen to the album, and leave comments on the review that they read. They can then post their own review of the album or create a review for a different album. Express, Mongoose, MongoDB, JSON, Heroku, mLab, React, Axios, node-spotify-api npm package, and Bootstrap were all used to complete this application.
